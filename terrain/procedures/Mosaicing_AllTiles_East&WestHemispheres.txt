#This script was used to mosaic tiles for final 90m DEM outputs, one for each 
# hemisphere.  Mosaicing was performed in five steps: 1) Mosaic Aster GDEM2 5 x 5
# tiles from N60 to N83 for each hemisphere, 2) Mosaic SRTM 5 x 5 tiles from S60 to
# N55 for each hemisphere, 3) translate blended SRTM/Aster tiles from Float 32
# (output from R script for Gaussian blending) to Int16, 4) Mosaic blended 
# SRTM/Aster from N59 to N60 with SRTM from N55 to N59 (processing of tiles for
# blending procedure, including clipping of SRTM 5 x 5 tiles from N55 to N60 can be
# found in /organisms/DEM/DEM_ProcessingScripts/SRTM_ClipToN59to60.txt), and 4)
# Mosaic products from steps 1 through 3 into complete 90m grids, one for each
# hemisphere.

#Natalie Robinson 
#Created on Feb. 3, 2012
